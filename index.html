About Techshastra

TECHSHASTRA: Founded in 2023 by Dr. Sandeep Negi, TECHSHASTRA is the premier technical club at our college dedicated to incubating and executing innovative ideas related to technology. Our primary mission is to foster a collaborative environment where students can engage in activities such as technical quizzes, hackathons, coding competitions, workshops, and DIY innovation projects. We support and engage our members by organizing a variety of events that challenge their skills and promote continuous learning. Notable achievements include hosting a highly successful technical quiz, a presentation competition, and a hackathon.

vision


  1. Elevate TECHSHASTRA to an international level organization.
  2. Foster global collaborations and partnerships.
  3. Establish a reputation for excellence in computer science and cyber security.
  4. Inspire and empower students to innovate and lead in the tech industry.

mission


Incubate and execute innovative ideas related to technology. Organize engaging and educational activities such as technical quizzes, hackathons, coding competitions, workshops, and DIY innovation projects. Provide continuous support and learning opportunities for members. Cultivate a collaborative and inclusive environment for all tech enthusiasts. Facilitate skill development and practical experience through hands-on projects and events. Encourage and mentor students to achieve extraordinary feats in the technical field.
